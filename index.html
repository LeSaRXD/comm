Payment

All payment is done via PayPal invoices. That means you, the commissioner, don't need a PayPal account and can pay simply via debit/credit card.

All prices listed are in US Dollars, so is the invoice.

Payment can be done fully ahead of time or half-and-half. I do not accept full payment after completion.

What I will and won't draw

I will draw

  • Anthro characters (all species, including protogens, synths, etc.)
  • SFW or mildly suggestive themes
  • Pride flags, symbols, etc.

I might draw

  • Feral characters (Warrior Cat OCs, etc.)
  • Human-like characters with animal features (doggirls, catboys, etc.)
  • Candy gore

Please let me know if your character falls into these categories. I will only accept characters I think I will be able to draw well, since my skillset is limited.

I won't draw (TW)

  • Human characters
  • NSFW or nudity
  • Gore or blood
  • Fetishes
  • Offensive symbolism or actions (nazis, pedos, zoophilia, etc.)

Wait times

Whenever you reach out, I will tell you how many people are currently in queue, as well as the approximate wait time before I start working on your comm.

All commissions will be completed within a week (7 days) of staring them. For more complex commissions (like ref sheets), I will let you know beforehand if it might take me longer.

I will message you whenever I start working on the comm. The sketch will be done in 3 days or less, and I will send you the WIP for any potential changes or tweaks.

After completing the sketch (and tweaks) and getting your approval, I will finish the commission in the remaining time. After completing the drawing, I will send it to you for any final tweaks.

Changes and tweaks

I allow two changes/tweaks after I send the initial sketch to you. I only allow one batch of up to 3 tweaks after I finish the comm.

Any changes after the comm is complete will cost extra (around half the total price).

Each change/tweak will take up to 3 extra days to complete, but I will prioritize them and try to get stuff fixed as soon as possible.

Changes

Changes consist of any major tweaks to the pose, design, items present in the drawing, etc.

I only allow changes during the sketching phase.

Tweaks

Tweaks are minimal adjustments to the drawing, such as editing specific colors, adding piercings, minor patterns, etc. I also allow tweaking anything that was added after the sketch was done.

I allow tweaks both during the sketching phase and upon completion.

Commercial Use

I do NOT allow commercial use. You cannot use my art to earn money. Doing so will lead to me blocklisting you and sending a copyright strike. (Message me "you like reading TOS don't you" for a lil surprise)

Commercial uses include, but are not limited to:

  • Selling prints, designs, or merchandise containing my art
  • Using my art in Crypto/NFTs
  • Using my art to train AI models, even if you don't use them to generate profit

Refunds

The refund amount will not include PayPal fees.

You can get a:

  • Full refund if you cancel the comm before I start the sketch
  • Partial refund (full price minus the price of a sketch) if you cancel the comm after I started the sketch but before I started coloring/rendering.

I do not accept refunds after I have started coloring/rendering, or finished the commission.

I reserve the right to cancel and refund any commission if you:

  • Break my TOS
  • Act rude
  • Rush me to finish the comm before the established deadline

I will always inform before refunding and cancelling a comm. Most of the time, I will give a second chance instead of cancelling immediately. However, this is completely up to me and depends on how severe the violation was.

Miscellaneous

The finished drawing will be in 4096x4096 resolution, 1:1 (square) aspect ratio. If you want a different resolution or aspect ratio, please let me know before I start the drawing or during the sketching phase.

Upon completion, I only provide a full-resolution, uncompressed .png file. Image source can be provided (for extra cost) if requested.
